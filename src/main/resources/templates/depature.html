Sagarmatha Japanese Language Institute (SJLI) is located is Lagankhel-19, Lalitpur, Nepal. We provide excellent Japanese language courses to those students who are interested to study in Japan as well as those people who are trying to enter in Japan as Trainee worker. In the 21st century, under the basic idea that language acquisition is a way to communicate, SJLI’s goal is to turn out excellent human resource who can work and study in Japan and get back to their own country and serve as a skilled manpower and give contribution for country’s economic development.
