Qualification

  • Minimum qualification: +2/equivalent pass
  • Age Limit:
  • For +2 pass students
  • Minimum: 18
  • Maximum: 26
  • For Bachelor/Master pass students:
  • 30 or below 30 years will be accepted

Documents For Applications

  • All of original academic documents
  • Photocopy of original citizenship certificate and English translation of that
  • Passport photocopy if applicant have
  • Statement of purpose to study in Japan
  • Recommendation letter from school/college or working place which the student belongs
  • Gap certificate if the student has some gap in academic history
  • Other extra document may be required

Documents to certificate sponsor

  • Bank Balance certificate with statement of 3 years transactions
  • Annual income certificate for 3 years
  • Occupation letter if your sponsor is employee
  • Company registration certificate if your sponsor runs company by himself
  • Other supported documents to prove how income generated
  • Property valuation
  • Tax clearance for 3 years
  • Relationship certificate to make sure the relation between the applicant and its sponsor
  • Sponsor letter
  • Address Verification
  • And other extra document as per required
